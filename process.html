Process & Services

PROCESS

01. Discovery Call

We learn about your needs and address any inquiries you may have.

02. Site Visit

A visit to your home allows us to delve deeper into the project details.

03. Proposal Presentation

We provide a detailed proposal outlining our design fees after the site visit.

04. Design Concept

We develop mood boards, samples, or 2D layouts to establish the visual foundation for your project.

05. Detailed Planning

We finalize colour schemes, select hardware, specify furniture, and design bespoke elements.

06. Implementation Coordination

We assist with purchasing, contractor coordination, quality control, and styling for a seamless result.

07. Collaboration with Architects and Builders

We work with architects and build teams to ensure timely delivery, budget adherence, and quality.
